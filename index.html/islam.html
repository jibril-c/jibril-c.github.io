The Five Pillars of Islam

1. Shahada (Declaration of Faith)

Believing that "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is his messenger."

2. Salat (Prayer)

Performing five daily prayers facing the Kaaba in Mecca.

3. Zakat (Charity)

Giving a portion of one's wealth to the poor and needy.

4. Sawm (Fasting)

Abstaining from food and drink from dawn to sunset during Ramadan.

5. Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Making a pilgrimage to Mecca, if physically and financially able.
